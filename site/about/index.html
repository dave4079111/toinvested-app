Founder Story

Built by an Investor, for Investors

David J. Moore, MBA is a real estate investor, educator, and technologist with over 20 years of experience navigating the complexities of real estate, finance, and wealth building. Having worked across every stage of the investment lifecycle -- from sourcing and analyzing deals to renovations, legal structuring, and long-term portfolio growth -- David understands the challenges that investors face at every level.

After witnessing too many investors lose money on deals that looked great on paper but hid costly risks beneath the surface, David set out to build something different. The result is ToInvested: an AI-powered platform that delivers instant deal clarity, surfaces hidden risks, and empowers investors to make confident, data-driven decisions in under 60 seconds.

David combines his MBA-level business acumen with deep technical expertise in AI and machine learning to create tools that are not only powerful but genuinely accessible. His mission is simple: make real estate investing less risky and more rewarding for everyone.

Our Mission

Empowering Investors with AI-Driven Clarity

Every feature we build is guided by a single goal: help investors protect their capital, spot hidden opportunities, and build generational wealth with confidence.

Make Investing Simple

Strip away the complexity and jargon. Our tools distill the most critical investment metrics into clear, actionable insights anyone can understand -- from first-time investors to seasoned professionals.

Reveal Hidden Risks

Most deals fail because of risks that were invisible at first glance. Our AI scans for structural, financial, and market-level red flags that spreadsheets and gut instinct simply cannot catch.

Help Avoid Costly Mistakes

One bad deal can set an investor back years. We built ToInvested to be the safety net that catches potential disasters before they happen -- saving you time, money, and sleepless nights.

Provide Instant Deal Clarity

No more spending hours crunching numbers. Enter a property address and get comprehensive, AI-generated analysis in under 60 seconds -- complete with risk scores, projections, and clear recommendations.

Empower Users to Build Wealth with AI

Artificial intelligence should not be reserved for hedge funds and institutions. We put the same caliber of analytical power directly into the hands of everyday investors, leveling the playing field and creating a clear path to generational wealth.
