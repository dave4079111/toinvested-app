OUR STORY

Built by an Investor, for Investors

ToInvested was founded by David J. Moore, MBA — a real estate investor and technologist with 20+ years of experience. After years of analyzing deals manually with spreadsheets and fragmented data, David realized investors deserved better tools.

He built ToInvested to be the platform he wished he had when starting out: one that combines the power of artificial intelligence with decades of real investing experience. A platform where anyone can analyze properties, compare asset classes, and get personalized recommendations — in seconds, not hours.

Today, ToInvested serves thousands of investors making smarter, data-driven decisions across real estate, stocks, and cryptocurrency.
