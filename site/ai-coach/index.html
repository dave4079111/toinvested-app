I found a duplex at 123 Main St listed for $285,000. Good deal?
Great question! Let me analyze that for you. Based on the market data for that area: median rent for a 2BR unit is ~$1,250/mo. At $285K with 25% down, your estimated monthly cash flow would be $387/door after PITI and reserves. That's a 7.2% cap rate — above the 6% threshold I'd recommend. This looks like a solid deal worth pursuing.
What about the BRRRR strategy for this one?
For a BRRRR approach, if the property needs light rehab (~$25K), your all-in cost would be $310K. With an ARV of $340K, you could refinance at 75% LTV and pull out $255K — recovering most of your capital while keeping $774/mo positive cash flow. I'd recommend getting a contractor estimate first.
