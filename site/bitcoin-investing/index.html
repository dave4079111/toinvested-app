FAQ

Common Questions

How does the Bitcoin analyzer work?

Our AI analyzes Bitcoin price history, market cycles, on-chain metrics, and macro trends to provide risk scoring, allocation recommendations, and multi-asset comparisons against stocks and real estate.

Should I add Bitcoin to my investment portfolio?

Bitcoin can provide portfolio diversification due to its low correlation with traditional assets. Our analyzer helps you model different allocation percentages to see the impact on your overall portfolio risk and return.

How much of my portfolio should be in crypto?

Most financial advisors suggest 1-10% for crypto exposure depending on risk tolerance. Our AI helps you find the optimal allocation based on your specific financial situation and goals.

Is Bitcoin too volatile for long-term investing?

While Bitcoin has significant short-term volatility, its long-term returns have outperformed most asset classes. Our analyzer helps you understand the risk-reward tradeoff with historical data and projections.

How does Bitcoin compare to real estate as an investment?

Bitcoin offers high growth potential and liquidity but with significant volatility. Real estate provides stable cash flow and leverage benefits. Our multi-asset comparison shows you the complete picture.
