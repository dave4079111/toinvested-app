ABOUT DAVID

Investor. Educator. Technologist.

David J. Moore is a seasoned real estate investor and technology entrepreneur who has spent over two decades at the intersection of finance, education, and innovation. As CEO of YPN Inc, he leads the development of AI-powered tools that are transforming how everyday investors analyze deals and build wealth.

With an MBA and hands-on experience in residential and commercial real estate, David understands both the theory and the reality of investing. He's personally analyzed hundreds of deals and built a portfolio that generates consistent passive income.

His mission is simple: make professional-grade investment analysis accessible to everyone — not just Wall Street insiders.

THE VISION

Why I Built ToInvested

After years of analyzing deals manually — spreadsheets, late-night research, conflicting data — I realized there had to be a better way. The tools available were either too expensive, too complex, or too limited for the average investor.

I built ToInvested to be the platform I wished I had when I started. One that combines the power of artificial intelligence with decades of real investing experience. A platform where you can analyze any property in seconds, compare it against stocks and crypto, and get personalized recommendations from an AI coach that never sleeps.

Whether you're analyzing your first rental property or optimizing a multi-million dollar portfolio, ToInvested gives you the same analytical edge that professional institutions have relied on for decades — at a fraction of the cost.
