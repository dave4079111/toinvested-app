FAQ

Common Questions

How does AI real estate analysis work?

Our AI analyzes property data, comparable sales, rental rates, and market trends to provide comprehensive investment metrics including cash flow, cap rate, ROI, and an overall deal score.

Is the property analyzer really free?

Yes! Our core property analyzer, fix & flip calculator, BRRRR analyzer, and renovation estimator are all free to use. Premium members get unlimited analyses and advanced features.

What types of properties can I analyze?

You can analyze single-family homes, multi-family properties, condos, townhouses, and commercial properties. Our AI adapts its analysis based on property type.

How accurate are the AI projections?

Our AI uses real market data, comparable sales, and historical trends. While no tool can predict the future with certainty, our analyses provide a data-driven foundation for your investment decisions.

Do I need real estate experience to use these tools?

Not at all. Our tools are designed for both beginners and experienced investors. The AI coach can explain every metric and help you understand what the numbers mean for your strategy.
