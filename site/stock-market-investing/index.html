FAQ

Common Questions

How does the stock market analyzer compare to real estate?

Our AI compares total returns including dividends, appreciation, leverage effects, tax benefits, and risk-adjusted metrics between stock market and real estate investments over customizable time horizons.

Should I invest in stocks or real estate?

Both asset classes have unique advantages. Stocks offer liquidity and passive investing, while real estate provides leverage, tax benefits, and cash flow. Our analyzer helps you make data-driven comparisons based on your specific situation.

What stock market data does the AI use?

Our AI uses historical S&P 500 returns, dividend yields, sector performance data, and current market conditions to generate accurate comparison analyses.

Can I compare individual stocks against properties?

Yes. Our analyzer lets you compare specific stock tickers or index funds against real estate investments, showing side-by-side returns, risk profiles, and long-term wealth projections.

Is diversification across stocks and real estate recommended?

Most financial experts recommend diversification across asset classes. Our tools help you model different allocation strategies to find the optimal mix for your goals and risk tolerance.
